The Process

The Chitin Cycle

From shrimp shell waste to wearable artifact. Five stages of transformation.

01

Waste Collected

6 million tons of crustacean shells become global seafood waste every year. We start where everyone else stops.

02

Chitin Extracted

Shells are dried, crushed, and deacetylated into raw chitin flakes — the molecular backbone of every charm.

03

Biopolymer Formed

Chitosan transforms into a flexible, iridescent bio-leather sheet — the hand of luxury, the soul of compost.

04

Charm Crafted

Each piece is precision-cut and hand-stitched. No two are exactly alike — by design, by nature.

05

Returned to Earth

An accessory that returns to soil in under 12 months. The cycle closes. The story continues.
